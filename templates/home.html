Welcome to KidneyAI

KidneyAI is a smart diagnostic platform built to detect kidney cancer through MRI scan analysis. With the power of deep learning, it delivers results in seconds to assist medical professionals and save lives.

Our Mission

We aim to bring accessible, AI-powered kidney cancer detection to hospitals and clinics around the world. By enhancing accuracy and speed, KidneyAI supports doctors in diagnosing early, treating earlier, and improving patient outcomes. No setup required. Just upload, analyze, and act.
