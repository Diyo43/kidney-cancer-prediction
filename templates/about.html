About Our Platform

Our platform is built to assist medical professionals in early detection of kidney cancer using AI-powered image analysis. With a robust algorithm trained on thousands of MRI and CT scan images, the system provides fast and reliable predictions.

It is designed to be easy to use, accessible from anywhere, and capable of integrating into hospital systems. Accuracy, transparency, and accessibility are at the heart of this project.
